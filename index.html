What organizations actually do on Confani

Not just a tool.
An operating layer.

01 — HOSTING

Build the entire event in a paragraph.

Type or speak the description. AI parses speakers, sessions, time slots, room assignments, sponsor blocks. Speakers are auto-matched against existing profiles or invitations are drafted. Review side-by-side, edit inline, publish.

02 — ATTENDING

Multi-rep representation, done right.

Add as many representatives as you need with one click — like LinkedIn but for the event itself. The company appears once in the networking tab; each rep appears as themselves. Pay for "Featured" placement at the top of the list.

03 — REMEMBERING

Notes that flow upstream — automatically.

Reps log notes during and 72 hours after the event. Notes route to the company's event history — searchable, organized by event, owned by the company. Two years later, when a sales rep meets a contact again, the institutional memory is right there.

04 — MONETIZING

Sponsored visibility & in-session ads.

Pay for top placement in the networking tab — clearly labeled "Featured" for trust. Buy ad slots within session detail pages to reach the most engaged audience. Every click and every visit feeds your lead-quality dashboard.

05 — PROVING

Analytics with AI insights — auditable.

Pick a chart from 40+ templates. Filter by date, segment, event. The AI writes a narrative summary in your language — what changed, who showed up, what worked. Export to PDF for the board, or to your CRM for sales.
